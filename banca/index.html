Why choose Banca?

We strive to create global cryptocurrency community based platform. We use AI and expert system to achieve the dynamics of Banca's eco-chain and the automatic management. Big data analysis is utilized to deliver accurate services to various participants with specific needs.

Our Vision

In order to prevent the moral hazards existing in the centralized management of traditional investment banking, we have set up the business protocol, trust mechanism and reward system based on blockchain technology. Banca will combine people from all walks of life to build Wall Street for cryptocurrencies.

BANCA Token

Banca token fuels our community. Whenever a member would want to obtain services from any other member, he would give BANCA tokens in consideration of the services provided by such other member of the Banca community. We also established reward mechanism to encourage active contributions and participation
